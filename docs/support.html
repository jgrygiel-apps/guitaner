Frequently asked questions

The tuner isn't picking up my guitar

Make sure Guitaner has permission to use the microphone: open the iOS Settings app → Guitaner → enable Microphone. Play one string at a time in a quiet room, close to the microphone.

Does Guitaner work offline?

Yes. Guitaner runs entirely on your device and makes no network requests. No internet connection is required.

Which tunings and chords are supported?

Guitaner includes standard and many alternate tunings, a chord library, and a practice mode with progressions and a metronome. Your custom progressions are saved on your device.

Is my data private?

Yes. Guitaner collects no personal data and never records or transmits audio. See our Privacy Policy for details.
