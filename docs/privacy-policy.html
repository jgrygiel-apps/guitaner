Privacy Policy

Guitaner · Last updated 4 July 2026

Guitaner is a guitar tuner, chord and practice app. It is built to work entirely on your device. This policy explains what that means for your privacy.

We do not collect any data

Guitaner does not collect, store, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs. The app makes no network requests.

Microphone

Guitaner uses the microphone only to detect the pitch and timing of the sound you play, in real time, on your device. Audio is analysed on the fly and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

Data stored on your device

Your preferences — such as the selected tuning, sensitivity, and any custom chord progressions you create — are saved locally on your device so the app can remember them. This information stays on your device and is never sent to us or anyone else. Removing the app deletes it.

Children

Guitaner is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.
